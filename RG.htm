ICH Guidelines

The International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH) is an organization that brings together the regulatory authorities, academics, and pharmaceutical industry to discuss scientific and technical aspects of pharmaceuticals and develop ICH guidelines.

  • ICH E3. THE EXTENT OF POPULATION EXPOSURE TO ASSESS CLINICAL SAFETY
  • ICH E4. DOSE-RESPONSE INFORMATION TO SUPPORT DRUG REGISTRATION
  • ICH E6. GUIDELINE FOR GOOD CLINICAL PRACTICE
  • ICH E8. GENERAL CONSIDERATIONS FOR CLINICAL TRIALS
  • ICH E9 STATISTICAL PRINCIPLES FOR CLINICAL TRIALS
  • E9-R1_Step4_Guideline_2019
  • ICH E10 Choice of Control Group in Clinical Trials
  • ICH E17  Multi-regional clinical trials
  • ICH E20 ADAPTIVE  CLINICAL TRIALS (Concept paper, 2019)

More guidelines can be found on the ICH Official web site
